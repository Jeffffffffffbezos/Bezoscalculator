Joining a Trade Union

Trade unions advocate for workers’ rights, better wages, and safer conditions. For Amazon workers, joining a union can amplify your voice. Here’s how to get started:

  • Explore unions like Amazon Workers International or UNI Global Union, which support Amazon employees globally.
  • Contact local unions in your area (e.g., Teamsters in the U.S., GMB in the UK) to learn about membership and campaigns.
  • Attend organizing meetings or connect with coworkers to discuss unionizing efforts safely and confidentially.
  • Visit Union News for resources on workers’ rights and union activities.

Note: Unionizing can be sensitive. Research local labour laws and protect your privacy when organizing.
